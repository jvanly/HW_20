AI-Travel

AI-Travel App was conceived in hopes of helping travelers make plans that normally takes them between multiple conventional travel reservations platforms and the National Parks System's reservation system. By using AI, to run searches and make the best matches recommendation, you are sure to be on your way to another vacation that requires that elusive permit and availability, often via lottery.

The Problem

Vacation planning takes time in which people do not have. Finding time to take a vacation is already difficult, however, winning the lottery for a permit adds to the complexity of planning.

The Solution

AI-Travel App will provide travel options that fits your desired requirements, ordering them by the most matches to lowest matching.

User Insight

There are many reservation systems and travel websites available to travelers willing and able to do their own planning. For those with less time to travel, they also have less time to plan for a vacation, especially where it requires winning a permit to travel and to camp at popular locations within the American park system.

Ed needs a good travel planner who can get him a park permit(s) as well as plane tickets, hotels, and car rentals because of his limited time to travel and less flexible time to travel to remote and distant locations.
